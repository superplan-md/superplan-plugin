# Review Task Against AC Gotchas

Record recurring review and proof failures here.

## Add A Gotcha When

- the same stale-evidence trap appears repeatedly
- a repo-native verification flow looks strong but fails to prove the actual AC
- downstream tasks keep getting unblocked on weak proof

## A Surprise Becomes A Gotcha When

- it is likely to mislead future review again
- it reveals a repeated evidence-validity or freshness trap
- future agents would likely bless completion too early without the note

## Skill-Specific Vs Workspace-Wide

- skill-specific: the trap is about review judgment, evidence class, or completion authority
- workspace-wide: the trap belongs to the repo, product surface, or recurring verification behavior across skills

Workspace-wide traps should go to `.superplan/gotchas.md`.

## Keep Out

- one-off failed test runs
- generic advice like "verify carefully"
