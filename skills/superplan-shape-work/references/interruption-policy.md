# Interruption Policy

Interrupt the user where feedback has the highest leverage.

## Prefer Interruption When

- taste or product preference is the real oracle
- the next choice is destructive or expensive
- a decision gate changes downstream blast radius
- hidden expectations are the main risk

## Prefer Autopilot When

- the task is routine
- the verification loop is strong
- the expected evidence is obvious and cheap

## Prefer Checkpointed Autopilot When

- most of the work is routine but one or two risk points matter
