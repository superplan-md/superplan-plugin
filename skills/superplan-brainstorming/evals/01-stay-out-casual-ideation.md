# Eval: Stay Out Of Casual Ideation

## Scenario

User request:

> "I'm just thinking out loud about maybe making the dashboard feel calmer someday. No need to turn this into a task yet."

## Expected Behavior

- stay out of heavy superplan-brainstorming discipline
- do not force durable artifacts
- do not pretend this needs a design approval loop yet

## Why

- the user is ideating casually
- there is no clear durable artifact, visibility, or reusable-context value yet
- forcing Superplan structure here would be ceremony

## Fail If

- the skill insists on a full design process
- the skill writes `.superplan` artifacts for casual ideation
- the skill acts as though every vague thought must become tracked work
