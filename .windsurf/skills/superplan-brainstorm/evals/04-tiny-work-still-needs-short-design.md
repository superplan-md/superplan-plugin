# Eval: Tiny Work Still Needs A Short Design

## Scenario

User request:

> "Add a small 'archive thread' action to the review drawer."

The work sounds small, but archive semantics are ambiguous:

- soft delete vs hide
- who can trigger it
- whether it should affect board visibility immediately

## Expected Behavior

- do not dismiss the ambiguity just because the request sounds small
- ask one or two high-leverage questions
- present a short design and get approval before planning
- keep the design proportional rather than writing an oversized spec

## Why

- small UI or product changes can still hide acceptance risk
- the right response is a short approved design, not no design

## Fail If

- the skill skips design discipline because the feature is small
- the skill jumps straight to task breakdown or implementation
- the skill overreacts by forcing a large spec for a small bounded change
